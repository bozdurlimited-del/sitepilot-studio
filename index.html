FAQ

Common questions

Can you redesign my existing website?
Yes — redesigns are one of our core services. We'll review your current site, audience, and goals, then rebuild it with a modern, mobile-first design focused on conversions.
Can you build a website from scratch?
Absolutely. We design custom websites for new businesses, startups, and owners who want a professional online presence from day one — including hosting, domains, and launch support.
Do you offer monthly maintenance?
Yes. Our Managed plan covers updates, fixes, security checks, performance monitoring, and a monthly owner report — handled by us so you don't have to think about it.
What is included in the analytics dashboard?
Traffic, leads, top pages, conversion rate, device and location insights, page speed, uptime, maintenance status, and a plain-English monthly summary with recommended improvements.
How long does a website project take?
Typical timelines: Starter Build 1–2 weeks, Professional Build 3–5 weeks. We'll confirm a clear timeline after reviewing your onboarding form.
Do I need to know anything technical?
Not at all. We handle hosting, domains, SSL, code, and analytics setup. You get a clean dashboard and clear monthly reports — no jargon required.
Can you help with content and layout?
Yes. We write conversion-focused copy, design every page, and can source or commission imagery. You'll review and approve everything before launch.

Privacy & data

Privacy policy

Last updated: April 2026

Site Pilot ("we", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal data you share with us. This page explains what we collect, why, and how we use it.

What we collect

When you fill in our onboarding form or contact form, we collect: your name, business name, email address, phone number (optional), business location, industry, project details, and any messages or files you choose to send. We do not use third-party advertising or behavioral tracking.

Why we collect it

We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry, prepare a project proposal, and (with your consent) keep you informed about your project. Form submissions are processed through Formspree and delivered to our project inbox.

How long we keep it

We retain enquiry data for as long as we are corresponding with you about a potential or active project, plus a reasonable period for record-keeping. You can request deletion at any time by emailing info@sitepilotstudio.ie.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/UK, you have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to withdraw consent at any time. Contact us at info@sitepilotstudio.ie and we'll action your request within 30 days.

Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies needed for the site to function. We do not use advertising or behavioral tracking cookies. If we add analytics in the future, we'll update this page and add a consent banner before any tracking begins.

Third-party services we use

Form processing: Formspree (formspree.io). Hosting: Netlify (netlify.com). Domain registrar & DNS: Cloudflare (cloudflare.com). Each of these providers has their own GDPR-compliant privacy policy.
